Dear Residents,

Please be informed that the annual safety inspection and preventive maintenance for all elevators in Blocks A, B, and C is scheduled to take place next week. This is a mandatory safety requirement as per the state building regulations to ensure the smooth and safe operation of our lift systems.

Schedule Details:

  • check_circle Block A: Monday, Oct 30 (10:00 AM - 02:00 PM)
  • check_circle Block B: Tuesday, Oct 31 (10:00 AM - 02:00 PM)
  • check_circle Block C: Wednesday, Nov 01 (10:00 AM - 02:00 PM)

During the specified time slots, only one elevator will be operational in each block while the other undergoes inspection. We request you to plan your travel accordingly to avoid long wait times.

The maintenance team from Otis Worldwide will be carrying out the technical checks. Please cooperate with the security staff and technicians on-site.
